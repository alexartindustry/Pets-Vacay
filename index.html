About us

Its easy to see that animals are playing a larger role in our lives, and loving families are choosing to provide them with more options. At PetsVacay we put you in control. Find sitters, trainers, veterinarians, and groomers that best suit you and your pet.

Our Services

Dog Boarding

Your pets stay the night at the sitter’s home. A kennel free home like setting provides your pets with more comforts.
DOGGY Daycare

Drop your pup off for a fun filled day at a qualified sitter’s home.
House Sitting

A sitter will provide loving care in your home for your pets while you’re away.
Drop-in Visits

A sitter will stop by your house to feed, play with, and comfort your pets.

Dog Walking:

Your pup gets a walk around the neighborhood, or possibly takes a trip to a dog park near by.
TeleHeath:

Consult with a veterinarian to solve any issues your pet is experiencing.
Teletrainer:

Connect with a certified trainer to gain confidence and knowledge to better understand your pets.
Mobile Grooming:

Find a mobile groomer or mobile pet spa in your area.
