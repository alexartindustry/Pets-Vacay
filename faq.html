How do I join the PetsVacay team?

A

If you’re interested in joining PetsVacay to provide a service, you can sign up anytime.

Here’s how to get started:

  1. Go to PetsVacay.com and select Join The Team.
  2. Select your pet provider section (mobile grooming, teletrainer, pet sitter, or telehealth).
  3. Follow instructions to create your Service Profile.

How do I sign up?

A

If you’re a pet parent looking to book services with pet service provider in your area or online, signing up is free and easy.

  1. Go to PetsVacay.com.
  2. Select Sign Up
  3. Read through and agree to the PetsVacay Terms of Service.
  4. Follow instructions to create your Pet Parent Profile.

?

What are the service fees?

A

All pet service providers will be charged a 15% service fee on all bookings made through PetsVacay. This will be automatically deducted from your booking total after services have been provided.

For owners/pet parents, a $20 service fee will be added to each reservation. The service fee is refundable should the pet service provider or pet parent cancel the reservation. If there is a cancellation, the service provider's cancellation policy will also be taken under account.
